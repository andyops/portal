MMCOE Receives A++ Grade from NAAC

May 1, 2023

Mitra Mandal's College of Engineering, Pune was accredited with grade A++ by NAAC. MMCOE received 3.6 CGPA on a 4 point scale. The college is very proud of all it's members of Management, Principal, Teaching and Non teaching staff, Students, Parents and Alumni.
